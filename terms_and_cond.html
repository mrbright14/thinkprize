The Basics

ThinkPrize users are expected to have fun and to be safe, and those expectations influence how we advertise on our site. Advertisers must be honest, thorough, and exact about the products or services their ads promote; they must avoid content that misleads or offends; and they must never compromise our users' privacy. These Advertising Policies apply to all ads on ThinkPrize.

Advertisers must comply with ThinkPrize Terms and Conditions and all other ThinkPrize policies governing the use of our services. We may update our terms, policies, and guidelines from time to time, so please check in and review them regularly.

Our user base includes teenagers (as young as 16), nonagenarians, and everyone in between. Advertisers are responsible for ensuring that their ads are suitable for ThinkPrize users that are considered minors in the United States. They're also responsible for ensuring that their ads comply with all applicable laws, industry codes, rules, and regulations in each geographic area where the ads will run.

ThinkPrize may share information related to ads with third parties, including: (a) our media partners when your ads run in content related to that media partner; and (b) third parties whose products or services you’ve elected to use in connection with the ads.

Our users may share ads with other ThinkPrize users or save ads to their devices. They may use any of the tools and features we make available in ThinkPrize to apply captions, drawings, filters, or other creative elements to the ad. Age-targeted ads can be shared within ThinkPrize with ThinkPrize users of any age. To find out if you can restrict ad sharing and ad saving for your ads within ThinkPrize, please contact your account representative at advertise@thinkprise.com.

Advertising Restricted Content

Alcohol

ThinkPrize does not permit targeting ads that promote or reference alcohol to the following countries:

  • Bahrain, Egypt, Indonesia, Jordan, Kazakhstan, Kuwait, Norway, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Turkey, and the United Arab Emirates.
    Where ads that promote or reference alcohol are permitted, they must not:
  • Target or be likely to appeal particularly to minors.
  • Encourage or depict excessive consumption of alcoholic beverages.
  • Exploit an individual who is drunk or otherwise intoxicated.
  • Glamorize alcohol, or otherwise misrepresent the effects of consuming alcohol.
  • Associate alcohol with operating a vehicle or other activities that require a certain degree of skill or physical coordination, or with any illegal behavior.

Warning labels, such as “please drink responsibly” or its local variation (if applicable) must be prominently displayed in the ad copy.

Ads for alcohol products must be age targeted to at least 18+, or the applicable minimum drinking age in the country to which you are advertising:

  • Canada: 19+.
  • Japan, Thailand: 20+.
  • United States: 21+.
  • Sweden: 25+.
  • India: either 18+, 21+, or 25+ depending on the States or Union Territories being targeted.

Financial Products & Services

Ads for financial products and services must clearly and prominently disclose all applicable material terms and conditions to consumers prior to the submission of an application.

  • Ads for loans must disclose, among other things, APR, repayment period, fees and costs, penalties, and the contact information of the lending institution.

Ads for products intended for a limited audience should only be targeted to that audience. For example, if a credit card offer is limited to individuals over the age of 18, the offer’s ad campaign must be age targeted to 18+.

Ads for the following are not permitted:

  • Get-rich-quick or pyramid schemes or offers or any other deceptive or fraudulent offers.
  • Ads that promote particular securities or that provide or allege to provide insider tips.
  • Payday loans or predatory lending.
  • Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs).
  • Any other Financial Products and Services of questionable legality or other sensitivities, at our sole discretion.

Food Products

Ads for food products must accurately describe the qualities and characteristics of a food product, including any associated health and nutritional claims.

Our ad policies regarding the promotion of gambling apply to the promotion of online casinos, brick and mortar casinos, lotteries, and any product or service that asks ThinkPrize users to pay to play games of chance to win prizes with real-world value.

Advertisers must comply with all applicable licensing or registration requirements. Advertisers must provide ThinkPrize with proof of current license or registration. All gambling and lottery advertisers must be pre-approved by ThinkPrize.

ThinkPrize does not permit targeting ads for gambling or lotteries to the following countries:

  • Argentina, Bahrain, Colombia, Egypt, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and the United Arab Emirates.

ThinkPrize permits targeting ads for gambling and lotteries to the following countries:

Ads for gambling and lotteries must not:

  • Target territories where the advertiser is not authorized to operate.
  • Target or be likely to appeal particularly to minors.
  • Glorify gambling or misrepresent the benefits of participation.

Ads for gambling and lotteries must be age targeted to at least 18+, or the applicable minimum age in the country to which you are advertising.
